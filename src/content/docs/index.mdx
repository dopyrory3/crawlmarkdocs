## What Crawlmark measures

Every scan produces two independent scores and a stance classification.

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Open Score (0–100)" icon="approve-check">
    How well the site serves AI crawlers. Weighted across four layers: crawl access, renderability, structured data, and advanced signals.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Blocking Score (0–100)" icon="warning">
    How effectively the site blocks AI crawlers. Calculated from agent-level robots.txt rules, llms.txt presence, and WAF detection.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stance" icon="information">
    OPEN / MIXED / BLOCKING / UNKNOWN - derived from the ratio of blocked to total known AI agents in robots.txt.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Per-check statuses" icon="list-format">
    Each check returns `pass`, `warn`, `fail`, `info`, or `skip`. Only pass/warn/fail contribute to scores.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
